Research Support

Research Support Services is one of our prime focus and function of the Learning Resource Centre, dedicated to supporting the continued growth in our research capacity and maximising the impact of the research through knowledge transfer and related activities.

We engage ourselves in identifying the Research Funding Agencies, Fellowship and Scholarship Opportunities and communicating them to our research scholars for potential benefits.

  • Digital Library (Intranet Access only)
  • JUIT Publication Database
  • Open Access Journals
  • Inter Library Loan
  • Bibliographic Databases
  • Fellowship/Scholarship
