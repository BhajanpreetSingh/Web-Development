Five Regions of Responsibility for the Office of Student Affairs

  • Campus Life and Activities
  • Admissions and Guidance for Students
  • Alumni Relations
  • Diversity
  • Student Health and Wellness

While the number of different services and discrete administrative positions that make up the student affairs office varies based on the size of an educational institution, every college and university in the U.S. has one. In fact, studying and improving the many functions and services these offices fulfill for students is an area of study in its own right. This article explores those services and areas of enrichment, providing some insight into the role these professionals play in the college experience for students across the country.
