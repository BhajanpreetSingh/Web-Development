BOWNESS SOCCER CLUB

Bowness Soccer Club is a volunteer run organization that has served the Bowness community for a number of years. A new group of volunteer parents and community members took over the board in 2020. Their vision was, and still is, inclusion, developing a love for sport, and connecting community members.

The Club serves the NW Calgary Soccer Community; our primary market is Bowness but we are proud to welcome kids from across northwest Calgary. Bowness Soccer Club strives to operate in all seasons, giving kids the opportunity to play recreational soccer within their community even when snow is on the ground.

Outdoor typically runs from late April to August depending on the age group. Indoor runs from September to March for our younger age groups.
